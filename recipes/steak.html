Steak

This is a simple, yet perfectly cooked steak.

Our steak is prepared with the perfect amount of seasoning for a King.

Ingredients

  • salt
  • pepper
  • fresh garlic
  • fresh thyme
  • butter
Steps
  1. season both sides well with salt and pepper
  2. add oil to pan
  3. place steak on cast iron skillet at high heat for 3 min each side
  4. Bring heat to low temperature
  5. add half of stick of butter, 2 garlic cloves, and thyme to pan
  6. base steak with butter until cooked to your perfection
