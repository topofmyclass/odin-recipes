Chicken Alfredo

A creamy and rich alfredo sauce on a bed of fetuccini pasta topped with premium chicken

Ingredients

  • heavy whipping cream
  • garlic cloves
  • salted butter
  • salt
  • pepper
  • garlic powder
  • tony's seasoning
  • romano cheese
  • parmesan cheese
Steps
  1. boil fetuccini
  2. melt butter over medium heat
  3. add garlic and cook for 1 minute
  4. add flour and cook for 1 minute while stirring
  5. add heavy creamy and stir
  6. reduce heat and bring to gentle bubble
  7. slowly stir in cheese
  8. add salt and pepper to desired taste
